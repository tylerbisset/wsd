What Makes Turtles So Awesome?

Turtles are one of the oldest reptile groups on the planet. They are older than lizards, snakes and even crocodiles. Turtles are part of the reptile order Testudines characterized by the shell they have that acts as a shield. The smallest turtles in the world only grow to four inches and the largest can reach close to seven feet and over 2000 pounds.

Fun Facts About Turtles

  • Turtles existed about 220 million years ago.
  • Turtles exist on every continent except Antarctica.
  • There are over 300 species of turtles, all of which lay eggs.
  • The shell of a turtle is made up of 60 different bones.
  • Several species of turtles can live to be over a hundred years of age.
